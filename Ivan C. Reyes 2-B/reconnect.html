Why Reconnecting to other people is important?

As social beings, humans have an innate need for connection with others. Reconnecting with others can provide a sense of belonging, reduce feelings of loneliness and isolation, and improve overall well-being.Reconnecting with others can have a positive impact on various aspects of your life, including your social, emotional, and mental well-being.
